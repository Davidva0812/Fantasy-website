Bard

The Gambler

The Bard is a master of rhythm, voice, manipulator of emotion, perception, persuasion and magical melodies. With their instrument in hand and stories in memory, they inspire allies and defeat enemies with their mystical performances. They're also cunning gamblers, skilled at sleight of hand, misdirection, and arcane card tricks that can influence reality itself.
