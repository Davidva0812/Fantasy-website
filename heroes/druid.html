Swarmcaller Druid

The Hive Mind

Deep within Eldoria's forests and forgotten groves dwell the Swarmcallers, druids who embody the wild's chaotic unity. Unlike other druids who call lightning or shape-shift into beasts, Swarmcallers guide colonies — wasps, beetles, mushrooms, birds — as extensions of their will. They are the voice of tangled roots and buzzing wings, serving nature not as a gardener, but as its most relentless defense.
