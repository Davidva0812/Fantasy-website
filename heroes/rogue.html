Rogue

The Shadowblade

Silent, swift, and deadly, Rogues are the unseen hand that tilts fate. They navigate the world through cunning and speed, whether as thieves, assassins, or scouts. In a world of monsters and mages, a well-placed dagger remains the ultimate equalizer. Trained in stealth, misdirection, and precision strikes, Rogues often draw upon Vitalis not for brute strength, but for unmatched agility and control.
