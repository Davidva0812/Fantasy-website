Cryomancer

The Masters of Frost

The Cryomancer is a powerful elemental mage who bends the unforgiving cold to their will. Cloaked in frost and mystery, they channel the essence of winter to immobilize, damage, and control the battlefield. Their presence alone can lower the temperature in the air, and their magic brings silence, stillness, and the sting of ice.
